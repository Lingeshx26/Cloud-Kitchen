Meet the Chef
Yagna Sri
Head Chef & Founder

Born and raised in Coimbatore, Yagna discovered her passion for cooking in her grandmother's kitchen, where every meal was a ritual of love and patience. After studying at the Institute of Hotel Management, Chennai, she spent five years mastering South Indian, North Indian, and fusion cuisines in top restaurants across Tamil Nadu.

In 2021, she founded Yagna's Kitchen with a simple dream: to bring restaurant-quality, home-style food directly to people's doors. Today, every dish that leaves our kitchen carries her personal touch — balanced spices, fresh ingredients sourced daily from RS Puram market, and recipes refined over years.
